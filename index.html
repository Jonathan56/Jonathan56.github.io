Here is a little bit of what I can do

The following graph shows an interactive CV.
A basic timeline has been used to place my project in time, and a force layout from d3.js holds bubbles representing my current skills. The size of each bubble is calculated as follow:

$$index = (expertise \times timeInUse){\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)}^{\frac{t}{half \, life}}$$

Where:

  • expertise is scored on a scale of 0 to 10
  • timeInUse is the number of year I practised on a skill
  • t is the time in year since last use
  • half life time of a skill considered as 4 years

This is a work in progress, I am sure the force layout could be used to emphasis relationships (send me your ideas).
